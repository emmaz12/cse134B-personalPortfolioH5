Hobbies

Crocheting

Last year, I learned how to crochet for the first time! I found out about a company called Woobles, whose mission is to create easy to learn, beginner friendly crochet kits. These kits include everything you need to crochet a small animal! Ever since, I have been making crochet animals for friends and family. It is a great way to destress during exam seasons and to kill some time when bored!

"The Woobles is actually about more than just crochet. It’s about proving to yourself that you can always learn something new. Changing how you see yourself and your potential to grow. And building the confidence that you can take on whatever comes your way." -Woobles Founders

Volleyball

I have played competitive volleyball for around 5 years before college in the AVA (Alberta Volleyball Association) league. Now at UCSD, I like to attend open gyms, play intramurals, or play for fun with friends! It is a great way to have come competitive spirit in my life and also its great exercise!
